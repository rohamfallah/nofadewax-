Our Story

How NoFade Wax was born in the heart of Dubai

Made for Riders, by a Rider

NoFade Wax was created by a young FlowRider enthusiast who noticed a problem: the wax he used gave good grip but left stains, marks, and faded patches on his beautiful board. It was frustrating. So he decided to fix it.

After experimenting with ingredients, testing at Wild Wadi Dubai every week, and improving the formula — NoFade Wax was born. A wax that grips strong but never ruins the board’s color. And the best part? It’s made right here in Dubai.

Built on Passion

This business isn’t run by a big company — it’s powered by creativity, action, and the love for riding waves. Each bar is handmade, packed carefully, and tested by real riders to make sure it works every time.

Whether you ride for fun or you're chasing tricks, NoFade Wax is here to help you grip better and ride smarter.
